OUR STORY

Ma never measured.
She knew.

A pinch more roasted cumin when the rains came. Curry leaves crisped in ghee, never oil. Pepper pounded coarse so it blooms in hot rasam. We bottled that knowing — not a recipe card, but a kitchen's worth of instinct.

Every jar of MA… starts as whole spices on a hot pan and ends sealed under a gold lid within hours — never warehoused, never stretched with rice flour or salt.
